Micropayment Apps

Case study

A mobile application with offline capabilities that enables field officers to record any cash payments that have been made. The mobile application generates receipt numbers that can later be used to identify each payment. It encourages accountability as the total amount of payments received from the field that day are clearly shown along with other payment details, which should match the amount handed over to the organization at the end of the day.

A web portal that is used by our client’s employees to manage and visualize data related to a farmer such as orders, farmer details, orders and repayments. The web portal also allows the client to generate reports and view trends within certain periods of time. It allows for offline capabilities whereby the portal can be used even in areas with poor network connectivity
